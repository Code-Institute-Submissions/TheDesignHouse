Colour Consulting

Instead of buying twenty sample pots to find the perfect shade to compliment your home, take the pressure off with a professional onsite colour consult. Based on our findings, we’ll recommend a complete colour scheme and provide you with charts and samples to help you visualise the final picture. We can even draw up electronic overlays of potential colour combinations to help you choose the one that best represents your personality.
