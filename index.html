Welcome to the Design House

At the design house, interior design agency situated in London-Knightsbridge, our priority is to take care of you, to know you and to listen to you, because the interior design must reflect yourself. We offer turnkey residential interior design services, interior architecture project management and furniture design for individuals. We will take care of every little detail, offering a stress-free service from design to project completion.

Why choose us

How do we claim to be the most complete interior design/furnish service? Your transformation journey will begin with a visit or book a consultation to our central location where you will be greeted by a senior member of our experienced interior design team. We will produce 2D&3D CAD drawings, mood boards and photo realistic. The Design house always really listen to their clients need to be it in terms of design, branding or full interior makeovers. We will objectively brainstorm until the right answer is found or the right look is in place
